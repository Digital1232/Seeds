Welcome

A Reunion Like No Other

Dear Alumni, it's time to relive the golden days of your college life. Alumni Homecoming 2026 is a celebration of the bonds you forged, the lessons you learned, and the dreams you chased — together.

Join us for a grand reunion filled with joy, nostalgia, and an opportunity to reconnect with your batchmates, professors, and the campus that shaped you into who you are today.

"Once an Einsteinian, always an Einsteinian. Welcome home."

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Alumni Homecoming 2026.

Any graduate or postgraduate alumnus of Einstein College of Engineering is welcome to register. Whether you passed out recently or decades ago, all Einsteinians are invited to come home!
The registration fee is ₹600 for batches 2008–2020 and ₹300 for batches 2021–2026. Family members (adults) may be added for an additional ₹300 per person. Entry is free for children.
Yes! You are welcome to bring your family. Please select "Yes" under the family option during registration and pay the additional ₹300 per adult. Children can attend free of charge.
Once you complete the registration form and submit your payment screenshot, your unique Registration ID will be displayed on screen. Please save it — further details will be shared via WhatsApp or email.
Registrations will close on July 20, 2026 or when seats are full — whichever comes first. We encourage you to register early to secure your spot and T-shirt size.
